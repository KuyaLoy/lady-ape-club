A Mystery Ticket allows holders to participate in various upcoming content. The tickets can be found in the Powder Room.

diamonds

LAC's unique utility token is the ‘Diamond’. These have no real market value outside of LAC. Holders can earn ‘Diamonds’ through missions and use them to purchase items through the LAC Shop.

DNA

A DNA is required to breed Baby Apes when used together with aJewelry Box. Each DNA is sold for 300 Diamond Tokens.

Breeding Baby Apes

Baby Apes are divided into three tiers: Silver, Gold, and Diamond. Tier Grades are given randomly when bred using ‘DNA’ and a ‘Jewelry Box’.

100 people holding BAYCs will each receive 1 LAC for free. Holders will be rewarded automatically with Super DNA.

Key

Jewelry Box

A Jewelry Box is required to breed Baby Apes when used together with DNA (similar to an incubator). Each Jewelry Box is sold for 600 Diamond Tokens.

Super DNA

Super DNA has a higher chance to breed high-tier Baby Apes and this is only offered to BAYC holders. Breeding can be done after purchasing a Jewelry Box required for breeding.
